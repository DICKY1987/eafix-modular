## 💡 **Implementation Tips**

### **Responsive Layout**
- Use **splitter windows** so users can resize panels
- **Collapsible sections** for less important info
- **Tab sub-panels** if space gets cramped

### **Performance Considerations**
- **Virtual scrolling** for large probability tables
- **Lazy loading** of historical data
- **Debounced updates** (don't refresh on every tick)

### **User Experience**
- **Tooltips** explaining each metric
- **Right-click menus** for quick actions
- **Keyboard shortcuts** for power users
- **Export options** for all data tables

This design balances information density with usability, providing traders with the critical strength and probability data while maintaining the professional feel of the existing interface.
